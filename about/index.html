OUR STORY

WHO WE ARE

Nilo Logistics is a dynamic, Kenyan-owned logistics and wholesale e-commerce firm built on the principle of seamless, transparent trade between China and Kenya. Founded to cut through the complexity of international shipping, we are your trusted partner for sourcing premium productsfrom the latest smartphones to high-quality office furnitureand delivering them directly to you at true wholesale prices.

Since 2010

Operating from Westlands, Nairobi (Regus – Delta Corner Tower 2, 13th Floor), we source wholesale from vetted suppliers, thoroughly test devices for A+ quality, and offer fast dispatch with packages ready at Yaya, Kindaruma Business Centre. Our expertise covers risk mitigation, compliance, and bulk deals, ensuring clients save time and avoid costly mistakes

    • Core Values

    • Integrity: Our word is our bond. We uphold transparency in pricing and processes.
    • Vetting & Quality: We ensure every device is thoroughly tested and every shipment meets the highest quality standards (A-plus certified smartphones).
    • Client Success: We are an investment in your success, focused on saving you time, money, and costly mistakes.
    • Priority Dispatch: We treat Your Cargo, Our Priority, ensuring fast and safe delivery, whether by Sea or Air.

    Our Mission

    To simplify international trade by providing deep sourcing expertise, rigorous product vetting, and rapid, secure shipping solutions (Sea & Air), thereby empowering Kenyan entrepreneurs and consumers with access to the worlds best products at unbeatable wholesale prices.

    Our Vision

    To be the most trusted and efficient logistics and wholesale e-commerce platform in East Africa, settin g the benchmark for transparency, reliability, and value in the China-to-Kenya supply chain.
